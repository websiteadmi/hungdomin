Included features to make things easy
Find your domain, create your site, and manage your online presence. All with Google simplicy and security.

Integrate Google products and partner services

Automatic domain verification

Integrate your website with other Google properties without needing to verify TXT records.

Google products built-in

Manage or add Google Workspace and view web traffic report in Google Search Console, all from your domains dashboard.

Auto-link with hosts and design platforms

Use your domains dashboard to auto-link your domain with one of our trusted partners Shopify, Squarespace, WIX, Bluehost, and Weebly.

Enhance safety with Google security

No cost WHOIS privacy

Protect yourself from spam and identity abuse by keeping your contact info private on WHOIS and RDAP.

2-factor authentication

Add another layer of protection with Google 2-Step Verification.

One-click DNSSEC

Safeguard your domains from threats like DNS spoofing and cache poison attacks.

Scale and grow with super-fast speed

Super-fast DNS

Scale to meet your website’s traffic demands and provides super-fast DNS resolution at no cost.

100 email alias and sub-domains included

With email forwarding, get up to 100 alias email addresses included with your domain. Design as many sites as you need with up to 100 sub-domains.

Fast multi-domain registration

Streamlined purchase and registration processes makes it easy to get multiple domains at once.

Collaborate and export to streamline work

Share domain control

Give clients or coworkers permission to manage a domain alongside you.

Share favorited domains

Favorite domains and share with others before purchase.

DNS export

Download your DNS records to standard formats like BIND or YAML for easy migrations and to upload to other DNS servers.
