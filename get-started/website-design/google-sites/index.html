Publish with your domain

Google Domains and Google Sites work together, so you can publish your website without technical know-how. Simply register a domain with Google Domains and publish your site to the web in just a few clicks.

Showcase your content

Easily embed a Google doc with your restaurant’s menu, your store’s physical location on Google Maps, or a YouTube video about your business. No coding skills required.

Collaborate and co-edit in real time

Google Sites makes it easy to collaborate with your team. Streamline your website updates with the same real-time collaboration and version history features you’re familiar with from Google Docs.
