Choose the right domain name
Keep it short and simple
When it comes to a domain name, less is more. Shorter, uncomplicated names are easier to remember.
Use brand and keywords
Using relevant words help users find you in a search. Your domain name should reflect your brand, service, or industry.
Consider alternatives
If your first choice is unavailable, don’t let that hold you back from starting. Use our search and suggestion tools to explore alternatives.
